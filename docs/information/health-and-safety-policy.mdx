## Venture Roleplay - Health and Safety Warning

Welcome to the Venture Roleplay community! Please take a moment to read the following health and safety warning before joining and playing in our modded GTAV servers.

### Disclaimer

Venture Roleplay is an online gaming community that hosts modded GTAV servers. By participating in our servers, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

1. Venture Roleplay and its staff members are not responsible for any incidents, accidents, or injuries that may occur while playing in our servers. It is your sole responsibility to ensure your own safety and well-being.
2. Users may experience fantasy violence, flashing lights, loud sounds, and other potentially disturbing or intense content during gameplay. Please be aware of these elements and exercise caution if you are sensitive to such content.
3. The modded GTAV servers may contain virtual representations of weapons, vehicles, and other items that may be used in-game. While these are purely fictional and intended for entertainment purposes, exercise caution and use them responsibly within the game environment.
4. It is important to maintain a healthy balance between gaming and real-life activities. Excessive gaming can lead to physical and mental health issues. Take regular breaks, stay hydrated, and prioritize your well-being while playing in our servers.
5. Venture Roleplay does not tolerate any form of discrimination, harassment, or harmful behavior. Treat fellow players with respect and follow our community guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for everyone.
6. Please note that the content and rules of our modded GTAV servers may be subject to change. Stay updated with community announcements, server updates, and rule modifications to ensure compliance and a smooth gaming experience.

By joining and playing in our servers, you accept that Venture Roleplay and its staff members are not liable for any damages, losses, or injuries, whether physical or psychological, that may occur during gameplay.

### Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or need assistance regarding health and safety matters or any other issues related to Venture Roleplay, please contact our community administrators or moderators. They will be happy to assist you.

**Important**: If you believe you are experiencing a medical emergency or require immediate assistance, please contact your local emergency services.

Thank you for your attention to this health and safety warning. We hope you have a great time in the Venture Roleplay community!
